Who We Are

Still Waters Band is a registered rock band from Gangtok,Sikkim with registration no:E21/GTK/2664 Vol.No 1 Law Department,Govt of Sikkim. Formed in July 2001 by NIIT students Ananth Pradhan and Suman Karthak and Sonam Lachungpa from Govt College Tadong. After series of line up changes over the years there are 5 current members namely: 1.Siddhartha Gurung on Drums. 2.Lokesh Thapa on Bass & Backing Vocals. 3.Subham Rai of RollHeads on Rhythm & Lead. 4.Abhisek Gurung on Vocals & Lyrics. 5.Sonam Lachungpa on Guitar & Chief Composer. Band music can be define as Riff Rock n Roll Band. The Band recently performed at Ziro Music Festival,Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh & Boomerang Rock Fest at Guwahati.
